\section{Part A — Admin Setup (Piyush Only, Do Once)}

\begin{notebox}
You need admin access to the Pollity Google Workspace. Log in with your admin \texttt{@pollity.in} account for these steps.
\end{notebox}

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{stepbox}{Step 1 — Enable Transcripts in Google Workspace Admin}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Go to \href{https://admin.google.com}{admin.google.com}
  \item Sign in with your \texttt{@pollity.in} admin account
  \item Left sidebar: \textbf{Apps} $\to$ \textbf{Google Workspace} $\to$ \textbf{Google Meet}
  \item Click \textbf{Meet video settings}
  \item Find \textbf{Recording} $\to$ toggle ON
  \item Find \textbf{Transcripts} $\to$ toggle ON
  \item Click \textbf{Save}
\end{enumerate}
\textit{Takes effect within a few minutes for all \texttt{@pollity.in} accounts.}
\end{stepbox}

\vspace{1em}

\begin{stepbox}{Step 2 — Create the Shared Drive Folder Structure}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Go to \href{https://drive.google.com}{drive.google.com} (signed in as \texttt{@pollity.in})
  \item Click \textbf{New} $\to$ \textbf{Folder} $\to$ name it \texttt{Pollity}
  \item Inside \texttt{Pollity}, create a folder named \texttt{Meetings}
  \item Inside \texttt{Meetings}, create four subfolders:
  \begin{itemize}
    \item \texttt{Monday -- SNVC Team}
    \item \texttt{Tuesday -- SNVC Class}
    \item \texttt{Thursday -- Product}
    \item \texttt{Friday -- General Team}
  \end{itemize}
  \item Right-click the \texttt{Pollity} folder $\to$ \textbf{Share} $\to$ add all \texttt{@pollity.in} team email addresses with \textbf{Editor} access
\end{enumerate}
\end{stepbox}

\vspace{1em}

\begin{stepbox}{Step 3 — Create a Meeting Notes Template Doc}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Inside the \texttt{Meetings} folder, create a new Google Doc
  \item Name it \texttt{\_TEMPLATE -- Meeting Notes}
  \item Paste the template from Section 4 of this document into it
  \item This is the master template --- do not delete it
\end{enumerate}
\end{stepbox}

\section{Part C — What To Do Each Meeting (Every Week)}

\subsection{Before the Meeting Starts (30 seconds)}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Open the \texttt{\_TEMPLATE -- Meeting Notes} doc
  \item File $\to$ \textbf{Make a copy}
  \item Rename it: \texttt{YYYY-MM-DD [Meeting Type]} \\
  Example: \texttt{2026-03-30 Monday SNVC Team}
  \item Move the copy into the correct subfolder (Monday / Thursday / etc.)
  \item Share the doc link in the team WhatsApp group so everyone can follow along
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{At the Start of the Call (30 seconds)}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Start Google Meet as usual
  \item Bottom right corner $\to$ click the \textbf{Activities} icon (looks like a star or three shapes)
  \item Click \textbf{Transcripts} $\to$ \textbf{Start Transcript}
  \item A notification appears: \textit{``This call is being transcribed''} --- everyone sees it
\end{enumerate}

\begin{notebox}
\textbf{One person only} needs to start the transcript (usually whoever creates the Meet link). Everyone in the call is captured automatically.
\end{notebox}

\subsection{During the Meeting}

Do not type detailed notes. Just jot down:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Key decisions (bullet points only)
  \item Action items as they come up: \textit{who + what + by when}
\end{itemize}
The transcript captures everything else verbatim.

\subsection{After the Meeting (5 minutes total)}

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Google auto-saves the transcript to the \textbf{meeting organiser's} Drive under \texttt{Meet Recordings}
  \item Move the transcript file into the correct \texttt{Pollity/Meetings} subfolder --- same folder as the notes doc
  \item Open \href{https://gemini.google.com}{gemini.google.com} (free, use your \texttt{@pollity.in} account)
  \item Paste the transcript text and use the prompt in Section 4.4
  \item Copy Gemini's output into the meeting notes doc under the \textbf{AI Summary} section
  \item Change the doc status to \textbf{Final}
  \item Post the doc link in the team WhatsApp group
\end{enumerate}

\end{tcolorbox}

\subsection{Gemini Prompt (Copy-Paste After Every Meeting)}

After the meeting, open \href{https://gemini.google.com}{gemini.google.com}, paste the transcript, and use this prompt:

\begin{tcolorbox}[colback=googleblue!8, colframe=googleblue, sharp corners, breakable, fontupper=\small]
\textit{``You are summarising a meeting for a startup called Pollity.in, which builds governance software for elected representatives in India. Read the following meeting transcript and produce:}
\begin{itemize}
\item \textit{A 3--5 sentence summary of what was discussed}
\item \textit{A bullet list of key decisions made}
\item \textit{A table of action items with columns: Item, Owner, Due Date}
\item \textit{Any blockers or open questions that need follow-up}
\end{itemize}
\textit{Be concise. Use plain English. Do not include filler or pleasantries.''}
\end{tcolorbox}

\section{If Google Workspace Transcripts Are Not Available}

If your Workspace plan does not include Meet transcripts (you will know because the Transcripts option will not appear in Activities), use \textbf{Otter.ai} instead. It is free.

\begin{greenbox}
\textbf{Otter.ai Free Plan:} 300 minutes of transcription per month. Your 4 weekly meetings average 60--90 minutes total --- well within the free limit.
\end{greenbox}

\vspace{0.5em}

\begin{stepbox}{Setting Up Otter.ai}
\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em]
  \item Go to \href{https://otter.ai}{otter.ai} $\to$ Sign up with your \texttt{@pollity.in} Google account
  \item Grant calendar access so Otter can see your Google Meet links
  \item In Otter settings $\to$ enable \textbf{Auto-join meetings} --- Otter will automatically join every Google Meet on your calendar and transcribe it
  \item After each meeting, Otter emails you the transcript and a basic summary
  \item Share the Otter link in the Drive folder alongside the notes doc
\end{enumerate}
\end{stepbox}

\vspace{1em}

The rest of the process (Gemini summary, Drive folder, naming convention) is identical to Sections 3 and 4.
